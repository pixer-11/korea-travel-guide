Wander Atlas is a curated, editor-reviewed resource for international visitors. We're transparent about how it's produced.

## AI assistance

Our guides are drafted with AI assistance and then checked against live data. We disclose this on every article. AI helps us keep coverage broad and up to date; it does not replace factual verification.

## Where our facts come from

- Business details, ratings, and hours: Google Places data.
- Practical info (visa, emergency, transport): official government and tourism sources.

## Our verification standards

Because coverage is broad, we hold every venue guide to concrete, automated standards rather than opinions:

- **Quality floor:** when verified rating data is available, we only feature places rated 4.0★ or higher with a meaningful number of reviews.
- **Freshness:** venue data is re-checked on a schedule; anything that has closed is automatically unpublished, so we don't send you to a place that no longer exists.
- **Licensed images only:** every image comes from an approved, licensed source (Google Places, Unsplash, or public/open data) with attribution — never a scraped photo.
- **No invented details:** our drafting process is explicitly instructed never to fabricate hours, prices, or addresses; specifics come from data, not guesswork.

Guides are produced by the Wander Atlas editorial team using this process. We do not claim personal visits we haven't made — we're a curation-and-verification resource, and we say so.

## Images

We only use images we are licensed to use — official Places photos, public-domain and openly-licensed sources (e.g. Korea Tourism Organization open data, Unsplash), or images we credit and link to their source. We do not republish others' photos without a license.

## Corrections

Found something out of date? Places close and prices change. We update automatically, but if you spot an error, let us know.
